Who I am

I am a 25 year-old student based in Paris, France. I joined the MSc Artificial Intelligence in 2023. Passionnate about deep learning and natural language AI, I hope to further my understanding of the subject during the coming years.

Past experiences

In 2021, I graduated from a Mathematics degree at Sorbonne Université in Paris. In the course of the following two years, I studied various AI-related programs offered by the EdX platform. Since 2016, I have also worked as a maître d'hotel, cocktail bartender and volunteer in health and safety education at various events. I also have, over the years, accrued some self-thaught programming skills in several languages such as C, C#, Java and Python.

My outlook on the program

Thanks to my studies and past experiences, I have some practical and theorical understanding of AI. I expect the program to help me formalise them, as well as acquire new skills and deeeper understanding of the matter. I am more interested in the theorical aspects of AI, rather than the buisness ones, but I understand them to be both necessary aspects of the field. While I do not expect the technical aspects to be too difficult for me, thanks to my previous experience with them, I look forward to learning the proper academic methodology for them, and in my future works as a whole.
